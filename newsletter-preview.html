All We Hold have been quietly building something real, and "How I See It" is their clearest statement yet. The track opens with a patient, almost cinematic build before settling into a warm indie-folk groove that doesn't rush to impress. Vocalist Nathan Stocker's delivery sits somewhere between confession and clarity, giving the song an emotional directness that sticks. Released today, this one's worth your time. Keep an ear on this band.
